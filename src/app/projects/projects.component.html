Fridge

Stack: HTML/CSS | Javascript | Node.js | Express.js | Microsoft SQL Server

The idea for Fridge came from working in a laboratory as a researcher. When doing experiments, particularly in biological research, there are nearly an infinite amount of chemicals and reagnets you will go through in order to achieve your research goals. Unfortunately this means that as a lab ages and these reagents accumulate in the fridge, keeping track of and finding older, possible very vital reagents becomes nearly impossible.

Fridge is a laboratory fridge organization web application. Users are able to create an account, create fridges, boxes, and of course reagents, and are able to search through their information as well.
